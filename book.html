An essential piece in your collection of coffee table books

“Man Ray: Prophet of the Avant-garde” is a catalogue on Ray’s works as an artist, and his influence on the Dada and Surrealist art movements.

Typographic Explorations

Central to the catalogue is the use of experimental typography, serving as a key visual element to engage readers and reflect Ray's avant-garde style.

Some spreads from the book

The 12x12" book was meticulously designed and assembled by hand, employing the Japanese stab-binding technique.
